⚙️ Cancellation Policies

Define how much refund attendees receive when they cancel before your event.

How policies work: Each event can have one cancellation policy made up of tiers. Each tier specifies a cutoff time (hours before the event) and the refund percentage users receive. When a user cancels, the system checks how many hours are left until the event and applies the most generous matching tier. If no policy is set, cancellations are not allowed.
